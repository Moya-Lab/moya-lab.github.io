# objective: formar capital humano avanzado en Informática Aplicada a Salud y Medio Ambiente

        
El programa forma investigadores autónomos capaces de aplicar herramientas computacionales de vanguardia — Biología Computacional, Química Computacional y Tecnologías Habilitadoras Digitales — para resolver problemas relevantes en salud o medioambiente, contribuyendo al desarrollo tecnológico y sustentable del país.

      


      
      

        
# research_lines = 3  |  moya-lab_line = "Biología Computacional" [LINE_01]

      

      

        

          
LINE_01 · ★ OUR LINE

          
🧬 Biología Computacional

          
Genomics, metagenomics, multi-omics integration and AI/ML applied to microbial systems. Focus on environmental and health problems using bioinformatic pipelines and computational models.

        

        

          
LINE_02

          
⚗️ Química Computacional

          
Molecular simulation, ML-based force field generation, drug design, cheminformatics and medicinal chemistry. Bridging computational methods with molecular biology and pharmacology.

        

        

          
LINE_03

          
💻 Tecnologías Habilitadoras Digitales

          
Smart systems, IoT, deep learning for medical imaging (PET reconstruction), intelligent data architectures and real-time processing for health and environmental monitoring applications.
